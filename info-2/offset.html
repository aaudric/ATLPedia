Offset

Atlanta Rapper

Biography

Kiari Kendrell Cephus, known professionally as Offset, is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter. He is best known as a member of the hip hop and trap music trio Migos, alongside his relatives Quavo and Takeoff.

Offset was born on December 14, 1991, in Lawrenceville, Georgia. He grew up in a household of 14, with his mother and father being the primary caretakers. At a young age, he became involved in music and formed Migos with Quavo and Takeoff in 2009.

Offset has released several solo projects, including his debut album "Father of 4" in 2019. He is also known for his collaborations with other artists, including Cardi B, 21 Savage, and Travis Scott.
